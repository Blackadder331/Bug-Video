Mantis Infernalis (Hell Mantis)

A predatory insect that belongs to the order Mantodea and the family Mantidae. It is characterized by its large size, red coloration, and serrated forelegs. It inhabits volcanic regions and feeds on other insects and small vertebrates. It was first described by John Smith in 2020.
