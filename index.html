Erebus nocturnus (Nightmare moth)

A large and elusive moth that lives in the darkest parts of tropical rainforests. It has a wingspan of up to 30 cm and a black and purple pattern that resembles a human skull. It is active only at night and emits a loud screeching sound when disturbed. It feeds on nectar and sap from various plants. It was first described by Jones et al. in 2021.
